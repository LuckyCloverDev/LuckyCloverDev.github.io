As most people are aware, social media lately has been in a downward spiral, whether due to the algorithms or the company exchanging hands. This has been a problem for many people, especially artists who use these sites to find clients for their careers. In order to assist these wonderful artists, TofuPixel and other artists created a discord server named Café Dot for members of these social media platforms to join.

Café Dot has a thriving, active community of over 2000 members and is rapidly growing larger!

Café Dot has a few different mascots. The first and main mascot is Matcha, a friendly green cat who is an employee at Café Dot! They keep the café clean and cozy, and they help make the drinks to bring to any patrons of the café. Other than Matcha, there are no other character designs that are currently available to the public!

Come join Café Dot!
