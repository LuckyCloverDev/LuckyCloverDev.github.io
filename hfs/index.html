Housefire Studios is a game studio I started back in 2017. When it started out it was nameless, just me working on my own, learning to program and exploring my own interest in game development. As time went on, I brought on a few of my personal friends to help me and recruited a couple members from various places to help out. After awhile, these members eventually lost interest and left the team to continue with their own projects.

Once everything settled down, I was left with myself, Robin, B.R., and Chan as the members of Housefire Studios. I took the very first project I ever made, a game called "Lumpia and the Orb of Power" made in Scratch, and developed a story for it, redesigned the main character, and we began our work on the game that came from it: Shadow of a Doubt
